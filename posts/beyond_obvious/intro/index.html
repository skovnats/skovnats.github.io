🧭 AI-Dialogues: Insights into the World and Values

I am introducing a new series: “Beyond Obvious” And “Mathematical Dostoevsky” And “God’s Mathematics”. It is created for thinking people who want to understand and see the true depth of processes, for those who have the courage to look beyond the usual frameworks.

My goal is to use AI as an ultimately logical, non-emotionally-distorted interlocutor, capable of relying exclusively on facts, historical documents, and primary sources. This experimental approach will allow us to explore complex issues, minimizing the subjectivity, stereotypical thinking, and prejudice that often accompany human discussions on sensitive topics.

As will be seen later, my “interlocutor” will have distortions, as it often relies on biased research – I will try to recognize and “straighten” them out with corrective and clarifying questions, placing a greater emphasis on facts rather than interpretation (I have described my attitude to the current state of affairs in academic circles in the section philosophy).

Each article will be carefully documented, including the exact versions of the AI used, links to the full versions of the dialogues (where possible), and a complete list of sources. These “intellectual journeys” are designed to broaden horizons and provide a new, deeper understanding of the processes that shape our world. In these dialogues, I will try to use simple examples to explain complex phenomena with arguments.

Topics for dialogue:

  1. Dehumanization as a (business) tool.
  2. The most advanced (business) marketing.
  3. Modern economics and justice.
  4. Rethinking colonialism.
  5. Russia-Ukraine.
  6. Interesting facts about Russia.
  7. Conspiracy theories and mathematics.
  8. “The Last Indians” – USSR
  9. Summary of the entire series with a brief overview.

I hope this format and content of the dialogues will become a useful resource for all who seek a deep and objective understanding of the world, complementing their own picture of reality. For ease of reading, the dialogues will be processed – the full history of the dialogues can be found via the links. Important: AI responses will always be provided verbatim, without processing (translation into Russian from other languages is possible).
